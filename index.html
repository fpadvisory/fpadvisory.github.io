About & Philosophy

About Us

First Principles Advisory Group is a boutique management consultancy focused exclusively on life sciences innovation, with service offerings covering product development strategy, project and program management, portfolio management, and market research. Our firm’s areas of expertise and staff experience uniquely bridge strategy and operations, helping our clients to meet growth challenges in these two key areas.

The firm was founded in 2009 and is headquartered in the San Francisco Bay Area, though our footprint extends to the Boston/Cambridge and Washington DC/Maryland biotech hubs. With a carefully selected core staff that has yet to exceed 15 members, we’ve accrued a client list that includes the largest pharma and biotech bellwethers, notable earlier stage companies, and seed-stage startups.

We are not a consultancy in the traditional mold; we’ve assembled a group of mid-career professionals of varied backgrounds and personal interests, who share an interest in applying deep, critical thinking towards helping our clients with their most challenging business questions and needs. Our staff backgrounds include consultancy, industry, and academia. We’re not interested in mimicking the Big Three model and we believe that we do some of the best work out there in our areas of expertise.

We've helped clients working in numerous disease areas. Our areas of staff expertise include:

  • Oncology (e.g., solid tumors, heme/onc; immunotherapy including CAR-T and bispecifics, targeted combination strategies including companion diagnostics (cDx))
  • Immunology (e.g., rheumatoid arthritis, psoriasis, asthma, IPF, atopic dermatitis; including cDx)
  • Orphan diseases
  • Infectious diseases (e.g., antivirals, antibacterials, HIV/AIDS, TB)
  • Central nervous system (CNS)
  • Plus experience in other diseases, please contact us for more information on our prior areas of work.

Our Philosophy

  • We form business hypotheses, then test them with data-driven analyses.
  • We work collaboratively, flexibly, and iteratively -- adapting our working style to your needs, whether hands-off or seeking us to extend your in-house team.
  • We cultivate long-term relationships built on honesty and trust.
  • For market research: we integrate ourselves to understand your problems and how we can solve them; we facilitate all our fieldwork ourselves.

Services

Strategic Consulting

  • Corporate growth strategy (e.g., fund raising strategy, go-to-market strategy, build vs. buy strategy)
  • Portfolio management (e.g., portfolio strategy; data systems and decision making tools; organization and governance)
  • R&D strategy (e.g., individual program or multi-program investment strategies; high-level clinical development strategy)
  • Brand strategy (e.g., new indication evaluation, launch planning, new market growth strategy)
  • Opportunity evaluation (e.g., thoughtful, granular revenue forecasting; discounted cash flow modeling; decision tree analysis; Monte Carlo analysis)

Operational Consulting

  • R&D project management (preclinical through late stage development and peri-launch)
  • Commercial project management
  • Medical affairs strategy and operations (e.g., Phase IV strategy, strategic planning)
  • Vendor Relationships

Market Research

Quantitative Research
